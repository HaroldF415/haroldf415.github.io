Splash Page and other goodies.

Posted on: August 24, 2015

Was looking to add more responsiveness to my site during the weekend, so I decided to ask a couple of my friends who use iPhones to test it out. Turns out that my #conIconsDiv on my home page render the icons list one on top of each other. Looking through various sources I found that the solution for this was to add: display: -webkit-flex. Thanks to a friend of mine I followed his advice to attend a meetup, so tomorrow I will be attending DigitalOcean & Squarespace meetup. Also during the weekend I managed to learn how to create a landing page. It was sort of scary at first because my first thought was how can I get a reasonable size picture that will load fast in any devide. At the end I decided to go with one size pic and use height: 100vh. This means Viewport-Percentage or Viewport-Relative lengths.
