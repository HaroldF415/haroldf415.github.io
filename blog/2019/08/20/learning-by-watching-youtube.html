Learning By Watching YouTube Videos.

Posted on: August 20th, 2019

My quest to become a Web Developer continues. Finding small chunks of time between working for Uber/Lyft full-time and being a stay at home dad to actually get some work done. It is not easy because some days don't go well for me and I have to put in more hours than I would like to. Thankfully my wife and family have helped me out alot by babysitting the baby from time to time. Cutting to the chase, this is just a small blog entry to update myself and my website on what I'm doing. I will continue this later once I've consumed some knowledge.

Learning the ins and outs of an IDE can be very complicated. I started programming with Notepad++ then moved unto Sublime3, then eventually I found out about Atom. All of these have been very helpful throughout the years I've programmed. But I feel more comfortable with Webstorm. I first learned about WebStorm through my friend called Steve.
